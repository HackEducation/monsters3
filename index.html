Once again, I have spent much of 2016 on the road, traveling and speaking extensively about education technology's histories, ideologies, and mythologies. The Curse of the Monsters of Education Technology, a sequel to my 2014 book The Monsters of Education Technology and to my 2015 book The Revenge of the Monsters of Education Technology, is a collection of about a dozen of those talks on topics ranging from teaching machines to the technlogical imperialism.

The book will be published at the end of December. This site will be updated with more details once I, ya know, finish the book.
